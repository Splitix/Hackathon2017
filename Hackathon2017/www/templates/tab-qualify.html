Step 1: Qualify!

The first step is to find out if you qualify for Affordable Housing.

Check your annual household income against the latest income limits for HOME and CDBG respectively set by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD). The numbers are based on the HUD median family income (called MFI for short) in the Austin-Round Rock-San Marcos area. Learn more about the methodology for setting income limits, or read more about rent and income limits issued by the Texas Department of Housing and Community Affairs.
